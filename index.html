Shanti(シャンティースープカリー)

SHANTi was born in its hometown of Sapporo, Hokkaido, and has been loved by everyone as a popular soup curry restaurant in Harajuku, Shibuya, Ikebukuro, and Takadanobaba.
A soup curry that can't be imitated anywhere else, with a mixture of over 20 types of spices, a healthy soup with a highly medicinal effect, back fat added to give it a rich and rich flavor, and the simmered chicken melts in your mouth. Please try it at your local SHANTi.

A few highlights from our menu

From comforting curries to flavorful dumplings, each dish is made with care using fresh, seasonal ingredients. We cater to all dietary preferences and here's some of our customers favorites.

Nepali Thali Set

Centered with fluffy steamed rice, it’s accompanied by creamy black lentil soup (dal), spiced meat curry, sautéed seasonal greens, tangy pickles, and a crisp papad for added crunch.

Momo

Served with a rich, spicy dipping sauce, these bite-sized delights are perfect for savoring authentic Himalayan flavors.

Mandala Special Curry

A rich and aromatic curry with tender meat, fresh vegetables, and bold spices, offering warmth and comfort in every bite.

Chicken & Vegetable Soup Curry

Featuring a tender chicken leg simmered to perfection, assorted fresh vegetables like lotus root, baby corn, and eggplant, all bathed in a savory, mildly spicy broth.
